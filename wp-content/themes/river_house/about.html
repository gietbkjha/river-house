The Eastern Store

Located along the exquisite shores of the Eastern Shore, the Riverhouse at Easton is recognized as the region’s premiere venue for extraordinary waterfront special events, weddings and corporate functions. Our distinctive venue offers versatile event spaces, catering and dining options. Located on a golf course and country club, the venue sits in the midst of the Food Destination capital of the twin regions of St. Michaels and Easton, Maryland. Let us take the worry out of planning your celebration so that you can enjoy each moment with a venue offering breathtaking views, attentive service and award winning cuisine.

MISSION STATEMENT

To provide an exceptional experience for our guests that foster and promote lasting relationships in an environment that is dedicated to social responsibility and contribution to both communities and environment. We our core focus is service.
